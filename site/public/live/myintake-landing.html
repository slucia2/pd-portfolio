How it works

Three steps. Takes about five minutes.

Step 1

Build your profile

Add your medicines, allergies, conditions, and doctors. You can scan a pill bottle to fill in medications automatically.

Step 2

Show the QR code at any visit

At a clinic, pharmacy, or ER — open MyIntake, tap Share, and let them scan. No login needed on their end.

Step 3

Keep it current

MyIntake reminds you to review your profile regularly. Providers always see when each item was last confirmed.
